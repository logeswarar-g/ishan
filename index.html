Admissions

Join Our Community

Excellence Academy welcomes motivated students who are eager to learn and grow. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our challenging academic environment.

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the online application form with all required documents.

2

Entrance Assessment

Students complete grade-appropriate assessments in core subject areas.

3

Interview

Families meet with our admissions team for a personal interview.

Decision

Admissions decisions are communicated within two weeks of completing all steps.
